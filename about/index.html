About SheEO Business Network Dubai

SheEO Business Network Dubai is a professional platform dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs, founders, and professionals through meaningful connections, strategic support, and collaborative growth opportunities.

Our mission is to create a supportive ecosystem where women can develop their businesses, expand their networks, and access the knowledge and resources needed to succeed in today’s competitive business landscape.

Based in Dubai, SheEO brings together a diverse community of entrepreneurs, consultants, creators, and professionals who share a commitment to innovation, leadership, and purposeful growth.

Through curated events, expert-led discussions, networking meetups, and collaborative initiatives, SheEO fosters an environment where ideas turn into opportunities and connections evolve into long-term partnerships.

"We believe that when women support each other, extraordinary businesses and communities are built."

Our Vision

To build one of the most trusted and impactful communities supporting women entrepreneurs across the UAE and globally.

Our Mission

  • Empower women entrepreneurs with knowledge and strategic guidance
  • Foster meaningful business connections and collaborations
  • Support personal and professional growth
  • Create opportunities for visibility and leadership

What We Offer

SheEO provides a range of initiatives designed to support women in business, including:

  • Business consultations
  • Personal growth and leadership programs
  • Digital marketing support
  • SME business guidance
  • Networking events and group meetups
  • Podcast and media visibility opportunities
  • Strategic collaborations

Our goal is to provide a platform where women can confidently build, grow, and scale their ventures.
